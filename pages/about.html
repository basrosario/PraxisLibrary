Our Mission

"The best way to predict the future is to create it."

We created Praxis because we believe that true innovation in AI isn't just about companies adopting AI as a new technology—it's about people learning about, adapting to, and adopting Artificial Intelligence into their daily lives to empower and unlock their own human potential.

Our mission is to demystify AI use and drive personal adoption, with the goal of creating collaborative environments where technology and human creativity combine to solve complex problems and build a better tomorrow.

Why We Built This

The Challenge

AI is transforming how we work, learn, and create. But too many people are left behind—intimidated by the technology or unsure how to use it effectively. We believe everyone deserves access to these powerful tools.

What We Believe

True innovation in AI isn't just about organizations adopting new technology. It's about people learning to communicate with AI in ways that empower and unlock their own human potential. AI should amplify creativity, not replace it.

How We Approach It

We teach methodology over templates. We believe in learning by doing. We prioritize security and privacy. And we make everything free and accessible because AI skills should be for everyone—not just those who can afford expensive courses.

What We Value

Accessibility

AI skills should be available to everyone, regardless of technical background or budget.

Security

We practice what we preach. A+ rated CSP, no tracking, no ads, no external dependencies.

Practicality

No fluff. Everything we teach can be applied immediately to real-world tasks.

Honesty

We teach both the power and the limitations of AI. Critical thinking matters.

Meet the Founder

Basiliso Rosario

Passionate about making AI accessible to everyone, Bas built Praxis to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI technology and everyday users. With a focus on practical methodology over hype, he's committed to helping people harness AI to solve real problems and unlock new possibilities.

Praxis represents the belief that the future of AI isn't just about what technology can do—it's about what people can achieve when they learn to work alongside it.
