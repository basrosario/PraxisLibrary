AI Superpowers by Challenge

Practical prompts for executive function, communication, reading/writing, and focus management

Executive Function Support

AI excels at the organizational tasks that drain neurodivergent energyâ€”task breakdown, prioritization, scheduling, and decision support.

Task Prioritization

Prompt: "I have these 8 tasks. Help me prioritize by urgency and importance, and break the top 3 into specific next actions I can start in under 2 minutes."

Time Blindness

Prompt: "I need to leave for a meeting at 3pm. Work backwards and tell me exactly when to start getting ready, including buffer time for transitions."

Decision Paralysis

Prompt: "I'm stuck choosing between these options. Create a simple pro/con table and recommend which to pick based on [my priority]."

Communication Assistance

AI can draft, refine, and check communicationsâ€”reducing the cognitive load of navigating neurotypical expectations.

Email Drafting

Prompt: "Draft a professional email to [recipient] about [topic]. Make it friendly but concise. I want to convey [key point] without sounding [concern]."

Tone Checking

Prompt: "Review this message I wrote. Does it sound too blunt, aggressive, or unclear? Suggest specific changes to make it land better."

Social Scripts

Prompt: "I have a [situation] coming up. Give me 3-4 conversation scripts for common scenarios, including how to exit gracefully."

Reading & Writing Support

AI transforms how neurodivergent users interact with textâ€”summarizing, simplifying, and assisting with written output.

Document Summary

Prompt: "Summarize this document in 3 bullet points. Then tell me the one thing I absolutely need to know and any action items."

Writing Assistance

Prompt: "I need to write about [topic]. Here are my messy thoughts: [brain dump]. Organize this into a clear structure with an intro, main points, and conclusion."

Simplification

Prompt: "Explain this complex text in simple terms. Use short sentences and concrete examples. Highlight the key terms I need to remember."

Focus & Sensory Management

AI helps structure work sessions, manage energy, and create systems for better focus and sensory regulation.

Work Sprints

Prompt: "I have 2 hours of work. Design a sprint schedule with timed focus blocks, specific break activities, and transition cues between tasks."

Energy Mapping

Prompt: "Based on my typical energy patterns (high morning, crash 2-4pm, second wind evening), help me schedule these tasks optimally."

Environment Setup

Prompt: "I'm sensitive to [sensory issues]. Suggest a workspace setup checklist and strategies for managing [specific challenge] during deep work."

Pro Tip

Universal Prompt Template

Copy and paste this:

"I'm neurodivergent and need help organizing my thoughts. Here's my brain dump about [topic/situation]:

[Paste your unorganized thoughts here]

2. Extract and prioritize action items
3. Highlight anything urgent
4. Suggest what to tackle first
5. Use clear headers and bullet points"
