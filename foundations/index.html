The Truth About Modern AI

Yes, today's AI systems understand natural conversation remarkably well. You can just type what you need and often get good results. But understanding why certain prompts work better than others makes you consistently effective—not just occasionally lucky. Foundations give you the mental models to diagnose problems and improve results when simple prompts fall short.

They're Not Competing

These frameworks overlap intentionally. They're different lenses for the same goal: clear communication with AI. Use whichever mental model helps you think most clearly for each task—or combine elements from multiple frameworks.
