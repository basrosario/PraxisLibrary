How It Works

80 questions. 8 levels. 3 lives. One goal: reach Legendary.

Quiz Rules

  • 3 Lives — You start with 3 lives. A wrong answer or expired timer costs one life. Lose all 3 and it's game over.
  • Timer (Legendary) — Levels 1-7 are untimed. At Level 8 (Legendary), a 15-second countdown appears for each question. If time runs out, it counts as a wrong answer.
  • Hints — Trade 1 life to eliminate 2 wrong answers, leaving a 50/50 choice. You must have at least 2 lives to use a hint.
  • Streak Bonus — Answer 10 questions correctly in a row and earn back a lost life. The streak resets on any wrong answer or timer expiry.
  • Your Level — When the quiz ends, your achieved level matches how far you progressed. Reach question 80 to earn the Legendary status!

The Eight Levels

Progress through increasingly advanced AI concepts

Level 1: Beginner

AI fundamentals — what AI is, chatbots, prompts, and verifying outputs.

Level 2: Good

Basic prompting concepts, understanding AI limitations, and fundamental principles.

Level 3: Pro

CRISP, CRISPE, and COSTAR frameworks. When to use each methodology.

Level 4: Expert

Chain-of-Thought, Few-Shot Learning, ReAct, and Flipped Interaction.

Level 5: Advanced

Temperature, Tree-of-Thought, RAG, system prompts, and prompt decomposition.

Level 6: Champion

AI agents, guardrails, model distillation, prompt leaking, and grounding.

Level 7: Master

IDEs, APIs, prompt chaining, combining frameworks, and advanced reasoning.

Level 8: Legendary ⏱️

10 random questions from a pool of 100 covering security, alignment, agents, and cutting-edge AI. 15-second timer. Different every time.
