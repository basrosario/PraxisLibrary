Which Method Should I Use?

Each method has its strengths. Choose based on your needs.

CRISP

Quick tasks, simple requests

Use when:

You need fast, focused results without complexity

CRISPE

Creative work, persona-based tasks

Use when:

You want consistent personality in responses

COSTAR

Professional content, detailed outputs

Use when:

Audience and tone matter significantly

ReAct

Complex problems, multi-step reasoning

Use when:

You need transparent, verifiable reasoning

Flipped Interaction

Personalized advice, complex decisions

Use when:

You want AI to understand your situation before advising

Quick Tips for Better Prompts

01

Be Specific

Vague prompts get vague results. Include details about format, length, and style.

02

Provide Context

Tell the AI who you are, what you're working on, and why you need this output.

03

Give Examples

Show the AI what good output looks like. Examples are worth a thousand words.

04

Iterate & Refine

Great prompts are built through iteration. Start simple, then add complexity.
