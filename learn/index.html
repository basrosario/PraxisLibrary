Quick Tips for Better Prompts

Universal principles that work with any framework

Be Specific

Vague prompts get vague results. Include details about format, length, style, and constraints.

Provide Context

Tell the AI who you are, what you're working on, and why you need this output.

Give Examples

Show the AI what good output looks like. Examples are worth a thousand words of instruction.

Iterate & Refine

Great prompts are built through iteration. Start simple, then add complexity as needed.
