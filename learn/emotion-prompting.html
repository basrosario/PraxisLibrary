Emotion Prompting

Add emotional context to prompts to enhance response quality and engagement.

The Concept

Psychology Meets Prompting

Emotion prompting adds psychological framing to requests, emphasizing importance, stakes, or emotional context. While models don't feel emotions, they've learned from human text where emotional stakes often correlate with more careful, thorough responses.

Why It Works

Training data contains patterns where high-stakes situations receive more detailed attention. Emotional framing may activate these patterns in the model's responses.

Simple Enhancement

Add phrases like "This is very important to my career" or "I really need your help" to standard prompts.

Techniques

Emotion Prompting Patterns
