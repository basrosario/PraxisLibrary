Why This Framework

Roles Activate Different Thinking Patterns

Different roles activate different patterns of thinking in the AI. A "devil's advocate" role surfaces objections. A "teacher" role produces clearer explanations. A "senior engineer" role goes deeper on technical details.

Direct Knowledge Application You're not giving AI new knowledge—you're directing how it applies what it knows
Perspective Shifting Get multiple viewpoints by changing roles on the same problem
Domain-Specific Vocabulary Roles bring appropriate terminology and communication style
Important Limitation

Roles influence style and approach, but don't give the AI knowledge it doesn't have. A "legal expert" role doesn't make the AI a lawyer. Always verify important information with authoritative sources.
