Zero-Shot Technique

Rephrase and Respond (RaR)

Ask AI to rephrase your question before answering for better comprehension.

The Concept

Clarity Through Rephrasing

RaR asks the model to first rephrase the question in its own words before answering. This forces the model to process and interpret the question more deeply, often catching ambiguities and ensuring the response addresses what was actually asked.

Why It Works

Rephrasing requires understanding. If the model rephrases incorrectly, you can catch the misunderstanding before getting a wrong answer. If it rephrases correctly, it's primed to give a relevant response.

The Pattern

"First, rephrase my question to ensure you understand it, then provide your answer."

Examples

RaR in Action

Prompt

"What's the best way to handle errors in async JavaScript?

Before answering, please rephrase my question to confirm you understand what I'm asking."

Prompt

"How do I improve my model's performance?

Rephrase this question first—note any ambiguities—then answer."

Benefit

The model might identify: "Performance could mean speed, accuracy, or resource usage. And 'model' could be ML model, data model, or business model."
