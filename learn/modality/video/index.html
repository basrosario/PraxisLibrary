Video Frameworks

6 frameworks — AI techniques for understanding, generating, and editing temporal media.

Overview

Video modality frameworks address the unique challenge of working with temporal media — content that unfolds over time. These techniques cover understanding existing video (through captioning, QA, and temporal reasoning) and creating new video (through generation and editing). As AI video models rapidly advance, these prompting strategies become increasingly important.
