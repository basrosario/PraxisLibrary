Overview

3D modality frameworks represent the cutting edge of AI interaction with spatial data. These techniques cover creating 3D content from text, understanding physical spaces, estimating human poses, and processing raw spatial data. As augmented reality, robotics, and spatial computing grow, these prompting strategies become essential tools.
