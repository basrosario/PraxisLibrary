Why This Framework

Examples Are More Powerful Than Descriptions

Examples implicitly communicate countless details you might forget to specify. Format, length, tone, level of detail, edge case handlingâ€”all transmitted through good examples.

Pattern Matching AI recognizes patterns in examples and extends them to new inputs
Implicit Communication Format, tone, and style are shown rather than described
Efficient Learning A few clear examples establish the pattern without wasting tokens

How to Use It

Best Practices

Guidelines for creating effective few-shot examples

Show Diversity

Cover different cases the AI might encounter in your examples.

Match Real Data

Your examples should resemble actual inputs you'll provide.

Include Edge Cases

Show how to handle tricky or unusual situations.

Consistent Format

Keep input/output structure the same across all examples.

Quality Over Quantity

2-3 excellent examples beat 10 mediocre ones.

Clear Separation

Label examples clearly so AI knows where each begins and ends.

Best Fit

When to Use Few-Shot Learning

This framework excels when showing is easier than telling

Format Matching

When you need output in a specific style or structure.

Tone & Style

Teaching a particular voice or writing style through examples.

Data Transformation

Converting data from one format to another consistently.

Classification

Categorizing items when categories are hard to describe.

Translation Tasks

Language translation or domain-specific terminology.

Quality Standards

When you want outputs to meet specific quality criteria.
