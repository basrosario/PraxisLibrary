How to Play

Master the art of specific prompts

1. See the Vague Prompt

Each round starts with a vague prompt like "Write a recipe" that needs more detail to be effective.

2. Play Clarification Cards

Select cards that add relevant details. Not all cards are relevant—choose wisely to avoid unnecessary fluff.

3. Watch the Meter

The specificity meter shows how your additions improve the prompt. Aim for 5 stars without over-specifying.

4. Submit & Score

Submit when you think the prompt is perfect. You'll score based on specificity and relevance of chosen cards.

What You'll Learn

Skills that transfer to real AI interactions

Identify Missing Context

Learn to spot what information is missing from vague requests that leads AI to make assumptions.

Prioritize Clarifications

Not all details are equally important. Learn which clarifications have the biggest impact on output quality.

Avoid Over-Specification

Too many constraints can limit creativity or make prompts unwieldy. Find the balance.

Match Specificity to Task

A creative writing prompt needs different specificity than a technical documentation request.

Practice Makes Perfect

Use what you learn here to write better prompts in real AI conversations.
