What is AI Readiness?

Being prepared to work effectively with AI systems

AI Readiness means having the skills, knowledge, and critical thinking abilities to use AI tools effectively and safely. It's not just about knowing how to write prompts, but understanding how to:

1

Communicate Clearly

Write prompts that get you the results you actually need, not just any response.

2

Think Critically

Evaluate AI outputs for accuracy, bias, and relevance before using them.

3

Spot Problems

Recognize hallucinations, errors, and limitations in AI-generated content.

4

Iterate & Improve

Refine your approach based on results to get better outcomes over time.
