Understanding AI Readiness

What Makes You AI Ready?

AI Readiness means having the skills, knowledge, and critical thinking abilities to use AI tools effectively and safely. It's about understanding how to get consistent, quality results.

Communicate Clearly
Write prompts that get you the results you actually need
Spot Problems
Recognize hallucinations, errors, and AI limitations
Think Critically
Evaluate AI outputs for accuracy, bias, and relevance
Iterate & Improve
Refine your approach based on results over time
