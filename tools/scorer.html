Prompt Scorer

Get instant feedback on your prompts. Analyze clarity, specificity, structure, and context to improve your AI communication.

How Scoring Works

Your prompt is analyzed across four dimensions

Clarity

How clear and understandable is your prompt? Measures sentence structure, word choice, and readability.

Improve it: Use shorter sentences, avoid jargon, be direct.

Specificity

How detailed and precise is your request? Looks for specific requirements, formats, and constraints.

Improve it: Add details about format, length, style, or examples.

Structure

How well organized is your prompt? Checks for logical flow, sections, and clear instructions.

Improve it: Use numbered steps, headers, or clear sections.

Context

How much background information do you provide? Evaluates whether the AI has enough context to respond well.

Improve it: Explain your situation, goals, and constraints.

Strong Prompt

"Context: I run a small e-commerce store selling handmade jewelry. Task: Write a 500-word blog post about email marketing best practices. Include 5 actionable tips with examples. Tone: Friendly and practical. Audience: Small business owners with limited marketing experience."

  • Clear context and background
  • Specific format and structure
  • Defined audience and tone
