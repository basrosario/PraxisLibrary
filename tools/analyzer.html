Level Up Your Score

Six proven strategies to transform basic prompts into high-performers

1

Set the Scene

+15-20% potential

Start with context. Tell the AI who you are, what you're working on, and why it matters. "I'm a teacher preparing a lesson for 8th graders" beats "Write about history."

Try: "I manage a remote team of 12 people across 3 time zones..."
2

Assign a Role

+10-15% potential

Give the AI an expert perspective. "Act as a senior financial analyst" or "You are an experienced UX researcher" shapes how it approaches your request.

Try: "Act as a nutritionist who specializes in plant-based diets..."
3

Be Specific

+15-25% potential

Numbers matter. "Write 300 words" is better than "write something short." Include format, length, audience, and any constraints that shape the output.

Try: "Create a 5-point bullet list, each point under 25 words..."
4

Set the Tone

+5-10% potential

How should it sound? Professional, casual, encouraging, direct? "Use a friendly but professional tone" gives the AI a voice to match your needs.

Try: "Write in a warm, conversational style that feels approachable..."
5

Add Structure

+5-10% bonus potential

Use numbered steps, section headers, or bullet points in your prompt. Structure breeds structure in the output. It also earns structural bonuses!

Try: "1. Introduce the problem 2. Explain the solution 3. Give an example..."
6

Include Examples

+5% bonus potential

Show, don't just tell. "Like this: [example]" or "For instance: [example]" helps the AI understand exactly what you're looking for.

Try: "Here's an example of the format I want: Subject | Action | Result..."
Aim for 100%+ (It's Possible!)

The analyzer can score above 100% for prompts with exceptional structure. Structural bonuses reward numbered steps, section headers, output format specs, examples, edge case handling, and quality criteria. A well-crafted prompt with multiple bonuses can hit 130%!
