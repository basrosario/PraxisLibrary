Prompt Analyzer

Get detailed, intelligent feedback on your prompts. Our analyzer detects framework elements by understanding content, not just keywords.

How Analysis Works

Intelligent content detection, not just keyword matching

Sentence-Level Analysis

Each sentence is scored against all element types. We detect context, instructions, and constraints even without explicit labels.

Content Understanding

"I'm a marketing manager at a startup" is recognized as context, even without a "Context:" label. We understand what you mean.

Position Awareness

Context typically appears first, constraints at the end. We factor in where elements appear for more accurate detection.

Confidence Levels

Not just "found/not found" but high, medium, or low confidence. We show exactly what triggered each detection.
