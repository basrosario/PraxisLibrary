Quick Reference

Keep these principles in mind

1

Be Specific

Instead of "make it better," say "make it more concise" or "add more technical detail."

2

Give Context

Explain who you are, what you're working on, and why you need this output.

3

Show Examples

If format matters, show the AI what good output looks like.

4

One Task at a Time

Complex requests often get better results when broken into steps.
