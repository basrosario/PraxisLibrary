Understanding the Settings

What temperature and Top-P actually control

Temperature

Randomness

Controls how "random" the AI's choices are.

At low temperature, the AI picks the most probable next word almost every time. At high temperature, it explores less likely options.

Top-P

Vocabulary

Controls how many words the AI considers.

At low Top-P, the AI only considers the most likely words. At high Top-P, it considers a broader vocabulary before making a choice.

Common Mistakes

What to avoid when adjusting settings

High Temp for Facts

Problem: Using high temperature for factual queries can lead to "creative" answers that sound confident but are incorrect.

Fix: Keep temperature low (0.2-0.4) for factual, technical, or code-related tasks.

Low Temp for Creativity

Problem: Using low temperature for creative writing produces bland, predictable, generic output.

Fix: Raise temperature (0.7-0.9) when you want variety, voice, and creative expression.

Changing Both at Once

Problem: Adjusting temperature AND Top-P simultaneously makes it hard to understand what's affecting output.

Fix: Start with temperature alone. Only adjust Top-P if you have specific needs.
