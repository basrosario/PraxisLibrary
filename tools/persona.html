How It Works

Building effective role definitions

Define the Role

Start with a specific role title. "Marketing Strategist" is better than "expert" because it establishes domain knowledge and perspective.

Set Expertise

Decide how technical or accessible responses should be. A beginner-friendly expert explains jargon; a deep expert assumes prior knowledge.

Choose Tone

Warm and friendly works for coaching; direct and formal suits professional reports. Match tone to your use case.

Add Traits

Toggles add specific behaviors: examples make concepts concrete, questions ensure understanding, caveats prevent overconfidence.
