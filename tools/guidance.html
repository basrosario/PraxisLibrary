Why Use a Framework?

Structured prompts get better results from AI

1

Completeness

Frameworks ensure you don't forget important elements like context or constraints.

2

Clarity

Breaking your request into components forces you to think through what you actually need.

3

Consistency

Using the same structure helps you learn what works and refine your approach over time.

Learn More About Each Framework

CRISP Best for task-oriented prompts with clear deliverables COSTAR Best for content creation with specific tone and audience CRISPE Best when you have examples of desired output ReAct Best for complex multi-step reasoning problems Flipped Interaction Best when unsure what you need or want personalized advice
